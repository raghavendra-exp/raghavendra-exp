> *"Make the world a better place to live."*

I'm **Raghavendra** — born in UP, raised across India through Kendriya Vidyalayas, shaped by the **Indian Air Force**, and now channeling **17+ years of discipline, HR mastery, and operational precision** into the world of technology and data.

I saw my first computer in **2009** — and it ignited a curiosity that never stopped burning. What started as fascination became a career pivot: from managing 750+ officers' records to **building the automated systems that do it**.

## 🏗️ Projects That Made an Impact

### 🔄 Pan-IAF Transfer Residence Automation
> **Stack:** Oracle SQL · PL/SQL · Oracle Forms & Reports

Eliminated 100% manual intervention in updating **residence end-dates** for personnel across all of IAF. Built with full audit trails, history preservation, and zero paper dependency.

---

### 📋 Automated Transfer Choice Collection System
> **Stack:** Oracle SQL · PL/SQL · Oracle Forms & Reports

Designed a quarterly, **trigger-based automated system** that identifies eligible personnel, opens choice windows on scheduled dates, collects preferences — all without a single manual touchpoint.

---

### 📂 Pan-IAF Work Experience History Vault
> **Stack:** Oracle SQL · PL/SQL · Oracle Forms & Reports

Built a longitudinal **service record repository** for every IAF personnel — enabling future analytics, planning, and policy decisions backed by clean historical data.

---

### 🎓 Online Learning Platform (MCA Final Project)
> **Stack:** PHP · JavaScript · XAMPP

Developed a full-featured e-learning platform for academic submission — laying the foundation for a future in full-stack development.
